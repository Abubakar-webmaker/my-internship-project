    Advantage

  • Corrosion resistance: Zinc protects the base metal by providing a barrier and cathodic protection to corrosive elements. Ultimate service life depends on coating thickness and the severity of the environment (Humidity, Salty).
  • This barrier effect is further improved if the metallic coating is painted
  • Zinc is sacrificial to steel. Therefore, the lifespan of a coating is proportional to the thickness of the layer applied.
  • Galvanized steel can be used in applications that require deep drawing and bending operations.
